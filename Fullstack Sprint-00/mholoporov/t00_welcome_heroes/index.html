What makes a superhero?

The Flash is known for his unique super speed abilities. He is able to travel at incredible speeds beyond the speed of sound and use this ability for a variety of purposes, including fighting evil, saving people, and even time travel. Flash can also create powerful shockwaves and even penetrate solid objects. His super speed ability makes him one of the most impressive and powerful superheroes in the comic book world.
